What is SoldinSight?

SoldinSight is a free Chrome extension designed to help anyone on eBay make informed decisions by providing instant access to sold listing history and accurate profit calculations.

Whether you're a casual seller clearing out unwanted items or a full-time reseller managing hundreds of listings — SoldinSight gives you the data you need to price confidently and sell profitably.

🎯 Our Mission: Make eBay market research accessible, fast, and privacy-focused for everyone.

Our Story

SoldinSight was born from a simple frustration: researching eBay sold prices was slow and tedious. Clicking through multiple pages, manually calculating fees, and keeping track of sold items took valuable time away from actual selling.

We built SoldinSight to solve this problem. Our goal was to create a tool that:

  • Provides instant access to sold history
  • Calculates profits automatically
  • Works fast with smart caching
  • Respects user privacy completely
  • Remains free for everyone

Technology

SoldinSight is built with modern web technologies and best practices:

  • Manifest V3: Latest Chrome extension standard
  • Smart Caching: Chrome Storage API — 24-hour TTL, up to 200 items
  • Security First: XSS protection, CSP, input validation
  • Rate Limiting: Respects eBay's servers
  • Modular Architecture: Clean, maintainable code
