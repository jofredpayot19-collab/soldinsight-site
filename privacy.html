🔒 Privacy Policy

SoldinSight Chrome Extension

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

🎯 Our Commitment: SoldinSight respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data. Everything happens locally in your browser.

1. Information We DO NOT Collect

SoldinSight does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal Information: We don't collect your name, email, address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers
  • Browsing History: We don't track which eBay listings you view or your browsing patterns
  • Purchase History: We don't collect information about your eBay purchases or sales
  • Financial Information: We don't collect credit card, payment, or banking information
  • Location Data: We don't collect your geographic location or IP address
  • Analytics Data: We don't use any analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry

2. Information Stored Locally

SoldinSight stores the following data locally in your browser only using Chrome's Storage API. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

2.1 Cached eBay Data

  • What: Public eBay sold listing information (item titles, sale prices, sale dates, seller usernames)
  • Why: To improve performance and reduce redundant eBay requests (80%+ cache hit rate)
  • Duration: Cached for 24 hours, then automatically deleted
  • Storage Limit: Maximum 200 items cached at once (oldest items deleted when limit reached)
  • Storage: Chrome Storage API (chrome.storage.local) - never transmitted anywhere

2.2 User Preferences & State

  • Disclaimer Acceptance: Timestamp when you accepted the eBay Terms of Service disclaimer
  • Extension Version: The installed version number, used to detect updates and show a one-time welcome message
  • Currency Exchange Rates: Cached exchange rates from the Frankfurter API (European Central Bank data), stored for up to 12 hours to avoid repeated requests
  • Why: To remember your preferences and avoid unnecessary network requests
  • Storage: Chrome Storage API — never transmitted anywhere

2.3 Fee Configuration Data

  • What: eBay fee calculation rules and rates (public information bundled with the extension)
  • Why: To provide accurate profit calculations based on current eBay fees
  • Storage Duration: Bundled with each extension version, updated with extension updates
  • Data Includes: Fee percentages, tier thresholds, category-specific rates
  • Storage: Chrome Storage API - this is public eBay fee information, not personal data

2.4 Usage Tracking (Calculator Rate Limit)

  • What: A list of recently viewed eBay item numbers and the timestamps of when you viewed them (e.g., item ID "123456789" viewed at 2:30 PM). No personal information is stored — eBay item numbers are public identifiers.
  • Why: To enforce a soft rolling rate limit of 50 profit calculations per 2-hour window. This is a courtesy limit stored entirely on your device.
  • Duration: Each entry is automatically deleted 2 hours after it was recorded (rolling window). No entry persists beyond 2 hours.
  • Storage: Chrome Storage API (chrome.storage.local) — never transmitted anywhere, never leaves your device.
  • Reset: Entries expire automatically. You can also clear all extension data via Chrome Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data.
  • Note: Sold history and related listings remain fully accessible even when the calculator rate limit is reached.

2.5 What We DON'T Store

  • ❌ Your eBay login credentials (we never see them)
  • ❌ Your purchase or sales history (only temporarily cached sold listings you view)
  • ❌ Your payment information
  • ❌ Your browsing history outside of eBay
  • ❌ Any personally identifiable information

3. Data Sources and Transmission

3.1 eBay Data Scraping

  • What We Access: SoldinSight accesses publicly available eBay sold item history pages
  • How: The extension makes HTTPS requests to eBay's public purchase history URLs (e.g., eBay.com/sch/sold)
  • Data Scraped: Item titles, sale prices, sale dates, quantities sold, seller usernames - all publicly visible information
  • Your Session: If you're logged into eBay, requests use your existing session cookies (managed by your browser, not by us)
  • Rate Limiting: A 1–3 second random delay between requests prevents overwhelming eBay's servers
  • No Account Access: We never access your eBay account settings, messages, purchase history, or payment information
  • Disclaimer: SoldinSight is not affiliated with eBay Inc. We access publicly available data only

3.2 To eBay

  • SoldinSight makes requests to eBay.com to retrieve publicly available sold listing data
  • These requests appear as normal browser requests to eBay
  • We don't modify or intercept your eBay credentials
  • A random 1–3 second delay between requests prevents excessive server load

3.3 No Other External Connections

  • Current Behavior: Extension uses only local fee configuration bundled with the extension
  • No Remote Data: All fee data and configuration is included in the extension package
  • Fee Updates: Fee configuration is updated with each extension version release
  • Privacy: No external API calls except to eBay.com domains for sold item data, and api.frankfurter.app for live currency exchange rates (no personal data transmitted)

3.4 To Third Parties

We do not transmit any data to third parties. Period.

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No data brokers
  • No external servers or APIs (only eBay.com for sold item data and api.frankfurter.app for currency rates)

4. Chrome Permissions Explained

SoldinSight requests minimal permissions:

4.1 Storage Permission

  • Purpose: Store cached data and preferences locally in your browser
  • Scope: Local storage only (no cloud sync, no external servers)

4.2 Active Tab Permission

  • Purpose: Access the current eBay listing page to inject our UI and fetch sold history
  • Scope: Only works on eBay.com and international eBay domains
  • Limitation: Cannot access other websites or tabs

4.3 Host Permissions (eBay Domains)

  • Purpose: Make requests to eBay to retrieve sold listing data
  • Scope: Only eBay.com, ebay.co.uk, ebay.com.au, etc.

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: All cached data is stored using Chrome's encrypted Storage API
  • XSS Protection: Enterprise-grade HTML sanitization prevents malicious code injection
  • HTTPS Only: All network requests use secure HTTPS connections
  • CSP (Content Security Policy): Strict security headers prevent unauthorized script execution
  • No Remote Code: Extension code is bundled and signed - no remote code execution

6. Data Retention

  • Cached eBay Data: Automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • Fee Configuration: Updated every 7 days (cached for up to 30 days)
  • User Preferences: Kept until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data
  • Manual Deletion: Clear all data via Chrome settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Cached images and files

6.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

Short Answer: Maximum 24 hours for most data, 30 days for fee configurations.

  • Sold History Cache: 24 hours maximum, then automatically purged
  • Request Timestamps: 1 minute only (for rate limiting)
  • Fee Data: 30 days maximum (public eBay fee information)
  • Disclaimer Acceptance: Permanent until extension uninstall (one timestamp only)

6.2 Automatic Data Cleanup

  • Old cache entries are automatically deleted when expired (24 hours)
  • When cache reaches 200 items, oldest items are automatically removed
  • No manual cleanup needed - everything is automatic
  • Uninstalling the extension deletes ALL data immediately

7. Children's Privacy

SoldinSight is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you are under 13, do not use this extension.

8. International Users

SoldinSight works on international eBay sites (UK, Australia, Canada, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Japan). All privacy protections apply equally regardless of your location.

Your data never leaves your device - there is no server-side processing or cloud storage.

9. Third-Party Services

SoldinSight integrates with two external services:

  • eBay.com: To retrieve public sold listing data (required for core functionality)
  • Frankfurter API (api.frankfurter.app): To retrieve live currency exchange rates for the built-in currency converter. This is a free, open European Central Bank data service. Only a simple rate request is sent — no personal data is transmitted. Rates are cached locally for 12 hours to minimise requests.

eBay does not receive any personal information from SoldinSight beyond what you would normally share when browsing eBay listings.

9.1 eBay Integration Details

  • What We Request: Public sold listing data (titles, prices, dates, seller usernames)
  • How: Direct HTTPS requests to eBay.com using your existing session
  • What eBay Knows: Only that you're viewing sold history (same as viewing it manually)
  • Rate Limiting: A 1–3 second random delay between requests to respect eBay's servers
  • No Data Sent: We never send your personal information to eBay

9.2 No Other Third Parties

We explicitly DO NOT use:

  • ❌ Google Analytics or any analytics platform
  • ❌ Advertising networks or trackers
  • ❌ Cloud storage services
  • ❌ External APIs (except eBay)
  • ❌ Social media integrations
  • ❌ Email marketing services
  • ❌ Payment processors (extension is 100% free)
